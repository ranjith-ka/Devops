# YouTube Channel Setup Plan

## 🎯 Phase 1: Pre-Launch Foundation

### Channel Identity
- [ ] Define your **niche/topic** — be specific (e.g., "DevOps for Startups" vs. "Tech")
- [ ] Choose a **channel name** — memorable, searchable, reflects your content
- [ ] Write a **channel description** (who you are, what you post, upload schedule)
- [ ] Define your **target audience**

### Branding & Design
- [ ] Design a **channel logo/profile picture** (800×800px recommended)
- [ ] Create a **channel banner/art** (2560×1440px, safe zone 1546×423px)
- [ ] Pick a **color palette & font** for consistent thumbnails
- [ ] Create a **thumbnail template** in Canva / Figma / Photoshop

---

## ⚙️ Phase 2: Technical Setup

### Google/YouTube Account
- [ ] Create or dedicate a **Google account** for the channel
- [ ] Set up [YouTube Studio](https://studio.youtube.com)
- [ ] Verify your account with phone number (unlocks custom thumbnails, longer videos)
- [ ] Apply for a **custom URL** (once you hit 100 subscribers)

### Channel Settings
- [ ] Configure **channel keywords** (Settings → Channel → Basic Info)
- [ ] Set default **upload settings** (title template, description, license, category)
- [ ] Add **links** (socials, website) to banner
- [ ] Set up **end screen template** and **card templates**

### Equipment & Software

| Type      | Budget Option               | Better Option                |
|-----------|-----------------------------|------------------------------|
| Mic       | Phone mic / Rode VideoMicro | Blue Yeti / Shure SM7B       |
| Camera    | Phone (4K)                  | Sony ZV-E10 / Webcam         |
| Lighting  | Ring light                  | Softbox kit                  |
| Editing   | DaVinci Resolve (free)      | Final Cut Pro / Premiere     |
| Recording | OBS (free)                  | Camtasia                     |

---

## 🎬 Phase 3: Content Strategy

- [ ] Plan your **first 10 video topics** before launching
- [ ] Decide on **video format** (tutorials, vlogs, shorts, interviews)
- [ ] Set a realistic **upload schedule** (1x/week is sustainable to start)
- [ ] Research **SEO keywords** using TubeBuddy or VidIQ
- [ ] Create a **content calendar**

---

## 🚀 Phase 4: First Videos

- [ ] Record a **channel trailer** (60–90 sec: what, who, why subscribe)
- [ ] Upload **3–5 videos before going public** (binge-worthy first impression)
- [ ] Write SEO-optimized **titles, descriptions, and tags** for each
- [ ] Add **chapters** (timestamps) to descriptions
- [ ] Create **playlists** to organize content

---

## 📣 Phase 5: Growth & Promotion

- [ ] Share on **LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Reddit** relevant communities
- [ ] Cross-post **Shorts** (vertical clips from your videos)
- [ ] Engage in comments — reply to every comment early on
- [ ] Collaborate with creators in your niche
- [ ] Build an **email list** from day one (link in description)

---

## 📊 Phase 6: Track & Improve

- [ ] Check **YouTube Analytics** weekly (CTR, watch time, traffic sources)
- [ ] A/B test thumbnails
- [ ] Aim for: **>4% CTR** and **>50% average view duration**
- [ ] Adjust content based on what performs

---

## 🔑 Key Tips

- **Consistency > Perfection** — publish regularly, improve over time
- **Thumbnails + Titles** drive 80% of your clicks — invest time here
- **First 48 hours** matter most — promote immediately after publishing
- **Watch time** is the #1 ranking factor — hook viewers in first 30 seconds
